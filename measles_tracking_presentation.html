Conclusion: Building Agile, Flexible, Resilient Ecosystems

The Measles Tracking Ecosystem as a Microcosm

Our measles surveillance infrastructure exemplifies the symposium's vision of cyber-physical socio-technical ecosystems that integrate:

  • Cyber space: Real-time data streams, automated processing, open APIs
  • Physical space: Health departments, patients, healthcare facilities
  • Hard domains: Infrastructure, sensors (web scrapers), analytics
  • Soft domains: Policies, regulations, skills, public health workflows
  • Multiple actors: States, counties, researchers, public
  • Agility & resilience: Adapts to changes while maintaining service
The future of digital health requires moving beyond technology implementation to ecosystem orchestration - designing systems that can navigate heterogeneity, adapt to change, and integrate social and technical components as interdependent parts of complex adaptive systems.
